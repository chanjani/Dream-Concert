Mat Kearney

Originally named Matthew William Kearney, Mat Kearney is an American-born rock, folk, CCM, and hip hop music singer born in Oregon. He began his career in 2003 after moving to Nashville, Tennessee and has never looked back - gaining recognition with his songs 'Nothing Left to Lose', 'Closer to Love', and 'Ships in the Night' - sample track.
