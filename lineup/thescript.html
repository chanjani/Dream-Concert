The Script

Formed in 2001 in Dublin, Ireland, The Script is a pop rock band made up of 3 members - Danny O'Donoghue, Mark Sheehan, and Glen Power. Their album, 'The Script', peaked at #1 in the charts in Ireland and the UK. They have become an international sensation with their hit songs 'For the First Time', 'The Man that Can't be Moved', and 'Breakeven' - sample track.
