One Republic

Formed in 2002 in Colorado Springs, Colorado, One Republic is an American pop rock band made up of 5 members - Ryan Tedder, Zach Filkins, Drew Brown, Eddie Fisher, and Brent Kutzle. Ever since then, they have become an international phenomenon with their hit singles 'Apologize', 'Stop and Stare', and 'Secrets' - sample track.
