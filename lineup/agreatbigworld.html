A Great Big World

Formed in 2011 at New York University, A Great Big World is an American music duo consisting of 2 members - Ian Axel and Chad King. In the beginning, they were featured in many shows like 'The Amazing Race'. Eventually, they hit the charts with their songs 'Say Something', 'Oasis', and 'This is a New Year', which was performed on 'Glee' - sample track.
